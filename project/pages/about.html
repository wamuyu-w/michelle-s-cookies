About Michelle's Cookies

From Campus Kitchen to Nairobi's Favorite Bakery

The Beginning:

Michelle's Cookies started as a passion project in 2020 while I was pursuing my Business Administration degree at Strathmore University. What began as baking treats for my dormmates during late-night study sessions quickly turned into a small campus business venture.

University Days

During my second year, I started taking orders for custom cookies and treats from fellow students. Word spread quickly about my unique recipes and attention to detail. Soon, I was the go-to person for campus events, birthday celebrations, and exam season comfort treats.

Growing the Dream

By my third year, the demand had grown beyond campus. I started participating in local food markets and events around Nairobi, building a loyal customer base. My business management classes helped me develop a solid plan for expanding beyond graduation.

The Bakery

After graduating in 2023, with support from family and the connections I'd built, I opened our first physical store in Kilimani Mall. Today, we're known for our artisanal cookies, custom celebration cakes, and innovative Kenyan-inspired treats that bring a taste of home to every bite.

Our Mission

To create delightful, high-quality baked goods that bring joy to our community while incorporating local flavors and traditions into modern pastry making.

Our Vision

To become Nairobi's most beloved bakery, known for innovation, quality, and bringing people together through the love of great baked goods.

Our Core Values

Quality

Using only the finest ingredients and maintaining the highest standards in every product we create.

Community

Creating a warm, welcoming space where everyone feels at home and part of our story.

Innovation

Constantly exploring new flavors and techniques while honoring traditional baking methods.
